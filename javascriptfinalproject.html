Final Class Project

A simple way for young kids to practice addition.

This simple app was designed as something a 1st grader could use to practice addition. The goal is to reach ten points. For every correct answer, a point is gained. Watch out though- a wrong answer will take a point away, and points are regained only by selecting correct answers. The whimsical outer space theme is a fun motif.
